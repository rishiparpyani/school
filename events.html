International Children Festival of Performing Arts (ICFPA)

International Children's Festival of Performing Arts (ICFPA) was started in the year 2000 under the aegis of ambuja Foundation. Its main aim was to create an international platform for young people to perform, unite, exchange ideas and explore the medium of dance, drama, puppetry and various other genres of performing Arts.We believe that performing arts is a sensitive, creative and liberal medium of expression which helps to establish a bond of friendship and cooperation among nations.ICFPA creates events and forums where participants from across the globe participate and amalgamate. The festival is not limited to viewing performances; here the focus is interaction, improvisation, play and discussion among the participants.The festival has spread its roots far and wide, with more Asian, African and European countries joining us year after year. In the year 2004, we had more than 280 performances and 4000 children participating in the festival.
